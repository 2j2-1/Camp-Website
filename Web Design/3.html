Style

Styles are what make a webpage look nice, this is slowly built up over time

Basics

Styles are applied to the text, they can apply different effects to each section of text, this can be from moving text around the page, changing color, size, font. Styles are applied tot the tags inside the <> eg <p style="font-size: 15px";>

Things To Try

  • background-color: #bbbcbf;
  • margin:10px;
  • padding:5px;
  • font-size: 30px
  • color:white;
  • text-align: center;
  • float:right;
  • text-align: center;color:white;font-size: 30px;border: 4px solid #ddd;

Task

Make your page your own, add colors, move things around this is where the creative side of website design begins
